Where to Eat in Downtown Wilmington



There are so many great options when looking for food in downtown Wilmington! With a deep history and hometown feel there is something for everyone!





  • Paddys Hollow

    • A wide menu of hearty American fare in a classic pub setting with an outdoor area facing the river.

    Menu

  • Pour Taproom Wilmington

    • Pour Taproom is a fun an exciting beer garden where you can try over 60 different craft beers and ciders with friends.

    Menu

  • The Copper Penny

    • Energetic saloon with nightly food & drink specials plus lots of microbrews on tap & in bottles.

    Menu

  • Fat Tony's Italian Pub

    • Pizza, other Italian eats & craft beers on tap in a casual sports-bar setting with outdoor seating.
